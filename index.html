About Me

I am Michele Castigliano, an independent freelance digital marketing specialist with over 10 years of experience helping businesses increase their revenue through data-driven strategies.

I specialize in 360° digital marketing — from paid advertising management across Google, Meta and TikTok, to website and e-commerce development, conversion optimization and advanced API integrations.

My approach is always results-oriented: every strategy is built around measurable growth, combining technical expertise with a deep understanding of each client's business goals. I work with a select portfolio of clients across e-commerce, fashion, food, and service industries.
