About Me

I am Michele Castigliano, an independent freelance digital marketing specialist based in Castellammare di Stabia (NA), Italy. I operate as a sole proprietor, managing paid advertising campaigns on behalf of a portfolio of individual clients.

My work is centered on managing Google Ads and Meta Ads campaigns for small and medium businesses across multiple industries including e-commerce, fashion, food, and local services.

For each client, I am granted administrator access to their own Google Ads account through my Google Ads Manager (MCC) account. I use the Google Ads API as a personal internal tool to automate reporting and monitor campaign performance across all managed accounts simultaneously — strictly for my own internal use, never shared with third parties or resold as a service.

My approach combines technical API expertise with practical marketing experience to deliver measurable, data-driven results for clients ranging from local businesses to growing e-commerce brands.
